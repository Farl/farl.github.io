# What to Eat?

A spin-the-wheel decision helper for picking the next meal.

- **Demo**: https://farl.github.io/what-to-eat/
- **Source Code**: https://github.com/Farl/what-to-eat

![What to Eat?](/img/portfolio/what-to-eat.png)

## About

Can't decide what to eat? Add a few options to the wheel and let it choose for you. A small but reliably useful "decide for me" tool.
