# 3D Physics Racing

Online multiplayer kart racing with browser-based vehicle physics.

- **Demo**: https://farl.github.io/3d_online_kart/
- **Source Code**: https://github.com/Farl/3d_online_kart

## About

A 3D online kart racer running entirely in the browser. Drive against other players in real time using a lightweight physics simulation.
