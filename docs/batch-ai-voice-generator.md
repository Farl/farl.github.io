# AI Voice Generator

Batch-generate AI voiceover from scripts with multiple speakers and styles.

- **Demo**: https://farl.github.io/batch_ai_voice_generator/
- **Source Code**: https://github.com/Farl/batch_ai_voice_generator

![AI Voice Generator](/img/portfolio/batch-ai-voice-generator.png)

## About

Paste in scripts, pick speakers and tones, and produce voiceover for many lines at once. Useful for quickly drafting narration for prototypes, game dialogue, or videos.
