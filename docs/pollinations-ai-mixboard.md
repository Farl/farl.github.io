# Websim MixBoard

A mood-board / canvas powered by Pollinations AI for live image and text generation.

- **Demo**: https://farl.github.io/pollinations_ai_mixboard/
- **Source Code**: https://github.com/Farl/pollinations_ai_mixboard

![Websim MixBoard](/img/portfolio/pollinations-ai-mixboard.png)

## About

A free-form canvas where you can drop prompts and watch them turn into images and text via Pollinations AI. Combine results into a mixboard for ideation and exploration.
