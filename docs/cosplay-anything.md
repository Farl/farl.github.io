# Cosplay Anything

Use AI to "cosplay" any subject as a different character or style.

- **Demo**: https://farl.github.io/cosplay_anything/
- **Source Code**: https://github.com/Farl/cosplay_anything

![Cosplay Anything](/img/portfolio/cosplay-anything.png)

## About

Drop in a photo and let AI restyle it as a cosplay of a chosen character or aesthetic. A playful experiment with image-to-image generation.
